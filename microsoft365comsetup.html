Microsoft365.com/setup

Go through microsoft365.com/setup webpage, Sign in, and enter the product key to download and install Microsoft 365 or Office on your computer. If you are using any other Microsoft service such as OneDrive, Outlook.com, Skype, or Xbox Live, it means you already have an account that you can use for any Microsoft activity. To download Microsoft 365 setup, go to microsoft365.com/setup and follow instructions.

If you bought Microsoft 365 online or in a store, you must enter your product key at Microsoft365.com/setup to redeem it. After you enter your 25 characters alphanumeric product key, you can download and install Microsoft 365 on a PC or Mac, or you can renew your Microsoft 365 at office.com/renew.


Microsoft 365 applications include:

  • Word — industry-standard word processor.
  • Excel — industry-standard spreadsheet application.
  • PowerPoint — industry-standard presentation software.
  • Outlook — industry-standard e-mail client.
  • OneDrive — cloud storage service on par with Dropbox, integrated with your Microsoft applications.
  • Skype — video conferencing, instant messaging, and international voice calls.

Microsoft Office Product Key – Where to enter MS Office keycode?

365 Office Key consists of 25 alphanumeric characters code and easy to register through the microsoft365.com/setup site. Purchase a keycode copy or buy a card, then check your subscription email or instruction side of the card showing the code as xxxxx-xxxxx-xxxxx-xxxxx-xxxxx. Once you locate the Microsoft 365 product key, visit microsoft365.com/setup and enter the code in the relevant box, then follow prompts to register the key.


Set Up Your Microsoft Account – Create an account & sign in

Microsoft365.com/setup – Microsoft Office login requires an account used in multiple activities like download, activation, renewals, and more. Follow the below steps to set up your Microsoft account;


  • Either use the Microsoft partner site or open the microsoft365.com/setup site.
  • Tap the “create a new account” tab. Hit “Sign In” if you are an existing user.
  • Once microsoft365.com/setup takes you next, use your outlook email or enter a mobile number.

Steps to Download Microsoft 365 (Microsoft365.com/setup)

  • Any web browser, such as Google Chrome, Microsoft Edge, or Safari, can be used.
  • Then go to microsoft365.com/setup and look for your product key in your order history. This is a one-time method to connect the purchased product to your account using the product key. Once the product key has been added to your account,
  • Tap the “Services & Subscriptions” tab at the top of the list. Then, on the right side of the office product, press the orange “Install” button.
  • The dialogue box will appear after that, and you must choose between “Download and Install” and “Save File.” Now press the Install button once more, and Microsoft 365 will begin to download. The file will be automatically saved to the download folder once it has been downloaded. To get the downloaded file, go to File Manager (Windows) or Finder (Mac).

Steps to Install/Setup Microsoft 365 on Mac

  • After the download is complete, launch Finder, navigate to Downloads and double-click the Microsoft Office installer. pkg file
  • To begin the installation process, select Continue on the first installation screen.
  • After reading the software license agreement, press Proceed.
  • Select Agree to accept the software license agreement’s terms and conditions.
  • Then select Continue after selecting how you want to configure Office.
  • You can change your install position or read the disc space specifications before selecting Install.
  • Then, if asked, enter your Mac login ID and password, and then select Install Program.
  • The installation of the program begins. When the installation is over, click Close.

How to Activate Microsoft 365 – (Step-by-Step Process)

  • To activate Microsoft 365 Setup, click here microsoft365.com/setup , find any of the Microsoft 365 applications, such as MS Word, Now open it.
  • This will open the Microsoft Excel application. Click on the icon which says activate Microsoft 365.
  • Enter your Microsoft email address associated with the Microsoft 365 and your 25 digit Microsoft product key to sign in.
  • This process will check if you have entered the valid Microsoft 365 details.
  • If everything is validated as per the requirement, your Microsoft 365 setup will be activated instantly.
