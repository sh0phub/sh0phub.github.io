How to create a Cricut Design Space Account ?

To use your Cricut Explore Air 2 or Cricut Maker machine, you will need to create a free account with Cricut. This is the name of Cricut’s software program that is used to create and design files your machine will understand.
Creating an account will give you access to your saved designs, projects, payment information and linked cartridges. It’s pretty easy to do too, let me show you all the steps.


  1. Open Cricut.com/setup.
  2. Enter your first and last name and email address, then select your country (United States, Canada, United Kingdom) from the drop down menu.
  3. If you are not in one of those three countries, select United States or the closest option to your true location.
  4. Read and review the Cricut terms of use and then check the box indicating that you have done so and that you accept them.
  5. Before submitting the information, consider the “Send me Cricut Tutorials, Inspiration and Deals via email” box at the bottom.
  6. Once you have that all set up, click Create User ID. You will see confirmation that your new account has been created, click Continue:
