Cricut.com/setup - What it is ?

Cricut.com/setup - A Cricut is an electronic cutting machine that can cut all sorts of designs from materials like paper, vinyl, card stock, and iron-on transfers. Some Cricut machines can even cut leather and wood! For your new Cricut, you will also need to have an online account ( cricut.com/setup ) so you can use Cricut’s Design Studio which is the operating software for Cricut machines. The software supports only newer machines which are Explore, Explore One, Explore Air 1 and 2 and new Cricut maker machine.


Guide to Set Up Your Cricut Machine on Printer - Cricut.com/setup

If you’re a craft person and are looking for new devices, then this post is for you. Cricut Machine is amongst the most advanced and creative devices existing. The machine can cut all kinds of designs from a variety of materials like vinyl, card stock, leather, and much more.

First, you create a design on the software or app exclusively provided by Cricut downloaded from cricut.com/setup . After the design is created, you simply send the design to Cricut cutting machine through Bluetooth or USB. On receiving the design, the Cricut Machine cuts the design with a small precise blade.


Here are the Steps You can Follow to Get your Desired Design Through the Machine.

  • Firstly, choose your cutting material and place it on the sticky mat of the Cricut Machine. As the mat holds the material in place to get a refined design out of it, load it in the machine.
  • Choose a design on Cricut software or app. Select the material settings and send the design to the machine.
  • Press the button on top of the Cricut machine and once the machine is done cutting remove the mat.

Moving On, Let’s See How You can Set Up Your Cricut Machine on Printer.

It is as easy as it sounds. The machine works exactly like a printer and hence it isn’t difficult to attach it with your printer.

Follow the steps below carefully.


  1. Before starting the setup process, download the Cricut Setup Software from the cricut.com/setup website.
  2. Sign in to your Cricut account. In case you are using the setup for the first time, make sure you create a new account and sign in.
  3. Select your Cricut device and click download.
  4. Wait until the setup is downloaded completely on your system.
  5. Next, double-click on the install.exe file to complete the installation.
  6. Once the setup is installed your Cricut Machine is ready for use.
  7. Plug in your Cricut Machine similar to what you do with a printer.
  8. Switch on the machine and connect it to your computer with a USB. Bluetooth is another way to connect to the machine.
  9. Now since you have Set Up Your Cricut Machine on Printer, you can enjoy creating designs and explore your creative ideas.

Here's Another method to Set Up Your Cricut Machine on a Printer is through your mobile.

  1. Assemble your Cricut Machine and switch it on.
  2. Use the Bluetooth option to pair your device with your mobile.
  3. Through your App store download the Cricut app and launch it.
  4. Sign in to the app with your Cricut ID.
  5. Click on the menu and select your device.
  6. Follow the on-screen prompts and get your desired designs.
